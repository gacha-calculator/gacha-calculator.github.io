What is a Gacha Calculator

"Can I afford this weapon and still get the char I want?" "How much should I save for 3 char dupes and a weapon?"

Stop guessing and approximating, that's what gacha calculator is for.

Getting started

First input or import your pity and other states like guarantee in the pity table

Then chart your pull plan, use + to make a new row if your pull plan is on a more complex side, you can do something like none -> c2, none -> r1, c2 -> c6, whatever your priorities are

Now if you press calculate it will give you a chart, up to 99.99% probability of getting your last goal

Below you can see that you can also pick either target probability(confidence level) or pull budget, importantly it calculates how many pulls x confidence level requires or what probability of getting everything you want you'll have with y pulls

Lastly it will also provide a detailed breakdown for this specific pull, probabilities of getting up to a certain point in your plan and cashback amount in pulls if game has such a mechanic

If you don't care about casbhack that's all you need, but if you do, you gotta fill/import constelation table, the amount of each character constelation of each rarity that gives cashback, it needs to sum to the amount of characters in this rarity, if you don't have enough on your acc, just add the lacking characters to none

You can also fill the rate ups if you know them, if you don't it's fine, but it will have to calculate with all possible combinations, decreasing the accuracy, you can fill it partially if you only know some from leaks!
